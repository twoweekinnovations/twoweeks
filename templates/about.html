About Two Week Innovations

Who We Are

Two Weeks Innovations is a project started by young entrepreneurs looking to revolutionize how tech products, and eventually products as a whole, are bought and sold. We cut costs through many ways to get products down from prices you may find elsewhere.

We buy and sell locally to support our community, and always keep the customer in mind. Product transparency is a massive part of our brand as well, and you can always ask about your favorite products before buying.

A huge cornerstone of our project is quality, we make sure our products havw 1:1 quality. We want our customers to be completely happy with our products.

We are on a mission to change the way electronics are sold, Two Weeks at a time.
